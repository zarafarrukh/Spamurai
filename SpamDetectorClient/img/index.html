About The Project

Welcome to our spam detector!

Fed up with the bothersome advertisements flooding your inbox? We've taken the initiative to develop a tool capable of sifting through spam emails. Our spam detector employs a unigram approach, analyzing individual words in an email to ascertain its spam status. By calculating probabilities based on word frequency, the program accurately distinguishes between spam and legitimate emails. To enhance its effectiveness, we trained the system using a dataset containing both spam and non-spam emails, implementing the SpamDetector class to facilitate model reading, testing, and training. With an abundance of samples, the program is well-prepared to effectively filter out unwanted messages, significantly enhancing the overall email experience.
